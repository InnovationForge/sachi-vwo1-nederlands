## 1. Key Theory

### Voorzetsel
A Voorzetsel (preposition) is a word that indicates a direction, place, or relationship¬π. You always use these words together with another word, which can be a noun, pronoun, or verb¬π. A Voorzetsel always expresses a relationship between the word group it is part of and another part of the sentence¬π. This can be the verb that stands before it¬π. Voorzetsels are almost part of a word group in which the main word is a verb¬π.

### Bijwoord
A Bijwoord (adverb) is a word that modifies a verb, adjective, other adverb, clause, or sentence¬π. Bijwoorden typically answer questions such as how?, in what way?, when?, where?, and to what extent?¬π. This function is called the adverbial function, and may be performed by single words (adverbs) or by multi-word phrases¬π.
