## 1. Key Theory

### Persoonsvorm (PV)
The persoonsvorm is always a verb‚Å∂. You can find the persoonsvorm in three ways‚Å∂:
- Change the sentence to a different tense‚Å∂.
- Change the sentence from singular to plural, or vice versa‚Å∂.
- Make the sentence a question, the persoonsvorm is then the first verb‚Å∂.

### Onderwerp (OW)
The onderwerp is the person or thing that the sentence is about‚Åπ. This part of the sentence always corresponds with the persoonsvorm[^10^]. To find the onderwerp in a sentence, you need the persoonsvorm. Then ask the question: Who/what + persoonsvorm?[^10^].

### Zinsdelen
Zinsdelen are different names for the same thing: you chop the sentence into pieces and give those pieces a name‚Åµ. A zinsdeel sometimes consists of 1 word, but can also consist of several words‚Åµ.
